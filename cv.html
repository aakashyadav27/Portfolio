About me

Proficient in tools like Dataiku DSS, Python, and various machine learning frameworks, I am dedicated to leveraging AI for innovative projects that enhance the efficiency and quality of clinical research.

My commitment to solving complex problems with AI aligns with my passion for delivering substantial value to clients and stakeholders.
